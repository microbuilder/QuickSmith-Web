Schematic: Converting Reflection Coefficients (Gamma) to Impedance and visa-versa

Converting Reflection Coefficients (Gamma) to Impedance

From the "Termination Menu" in the schematic chart choose Gamma. Drag "Gam" tool on the load and enter the gamma values. To convert to impedance, choose impedance from the "Termination Menu".

 

Converting Impedance to Reflection Coefficients (Gamma)

From the "Termination Menu" in the schematic chart choose Impedance. Drag "Resistance and Reactance" tool on the load and enter their corresponding values. To convert to Gamma, choose Gamma, from the "Termination Menu".
